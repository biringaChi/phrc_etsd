COMPUTER SERVICES

Process Computers

Process Computers unit uses Distributed Control System (DCS) to run the plant.
DCS are small computers distributed around the plant to control and monitor the plant.
There are basically four variables that are being controlled with different types of control system configurations.
These variables are level, flow, pressure and temperature.
For opening and shutting a valve, the Auto off and on control are used.
Level control employ basically proportion band control where the controlling signal is proportional to the change in the control variable within a set band. Flow and pressure control employ basically propotional plus intergral (reset) control. This helps to maintain smooth response of the control system for an upset in the control variable due to a sudden change in the load. Temperature control basically employ proportional band, plus integral derivative control (known as three term control). Unit task includes:

  • Maintenance and Management of DCS.
  • Carrying out daily system checks.
  • Troubleshoting and Recovery.
  • Carrying out system upgrade.
  • Taking records of daily system status.


User Support

This unit gives I.T support to all computer users in PHRC including hand-held devices.
User support staff are mainly responsible for the smooth running of computer systems and ensuring staff get maximum benefits from them.
Unit task includes:

  • Installing and configuring computer and peripheral hardwares, operating systems and applications.
  • Monitoring and maintaining computer systems.
  • Talking staff through a series of actions, either face to face or over the telephone to help resolve issues.
  • Troubleshooting system problems, diagnosing and solving hardware or software faults.
  • Replacing parts as required.
  • Following diagrams and written instructions to repair a fault or set up a system.
  • Administring lectures on the use of new applications.
  • Setting up new staff accounts and profiles and dealing with password issues.
  • Working continuously on a task until completion (or referral to third parties, if appropriate).
  • Rapidly establishing a good working relationship with customers and other professionals.

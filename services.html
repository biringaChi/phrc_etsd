SECTIONS IN ETSD

COMPUTER SERVICES

The Computer Service Section was established within PHRC in the year 1964. Over the period of years the Computer technology has advanced tremendously. This Section provides a complete package of Computer hardware and software, communication system, network and emergency services to staff within the refineries.

In order to offer optimum services to the user, Computer Service Section (CSS) has to match the hardware technological development within computing field with software developments for solving the User problems.
There are 4 units under C.S namely:

  • Application
  • User Support
  • IT Infrastructure
  • Process Computers ... More


GENERAL ENGINEERING

General Engineering is charged with modification, improvement and capital project execution, from initiation to completion.
And deals with technical evaluation of new technologies for use in the refineries.
There are 4 units under G.E namely:

  • Projects
  • Works
  • Plant Monitoring
  • Technical Library ... More


PROCESS ENGINEERING

Process Engineering monitors operating units and product quality and recommends corrective and improved procedures. Contributes to the development of new processes and procedures. They diagnose and troubleshoot processing issues. Identifies bottlenecks and debottlenecking opportunities.

P.E also monitors and optimizes unit operations and work processes in refineries and gas plants, Identifies opportunities for increased processing capacity.
There are 3 areas under G.E namely:

INSPECTION AND TESTING

Plant Inspection provides information about the quality and validity of static equipment in the plant.
The unit is responsible for carrying out inspection activities for both materials and static equipment in the plant. Inspection can either be done in the following ways.

  • Periodic External Inpection
  • Periodic Internal Inpection
  • Overhaul or Shutdown Inspection
  • Emergency Shutdown Inspection ... More

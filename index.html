Engineering and Technical Service Department (ETSD) is a department of the Technical directorate. A department of Port Harcout Refining Company Limited (PHRC) Charged with the responsibility of aligning Engineering / Technical Service with the evolving news of the business by cultivating the professional skills of the department. With a major function of rendering technical service mainly to the functional departments of the company

Another primary function is to serve as a prime source of technical information to the management on all technical matters. To achieve these functions, the department is charged with the responsiblity of:

  • Routine monitoring of the existing units and facilities.
  • Specifying various materials and equipment requred for PHRC operations.
  • Evaluating and modifying as necessary the existing Processes and Plants.
  • Evaluating and appraising new products and technologies as they apply to PHRC operations.
  • Investigating long term operating problems and objectives.
  • Carrying out Economic analysis as required; and mainltaining a Technical library.
